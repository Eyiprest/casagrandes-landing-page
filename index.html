About The Show

The Casagrandes tells the story of Ronnie Anne, a free-spirited and independent 11-year-old girl who moves to the big city with her family.

Meet the Family

The fearless and independent protagonist who's always ready for adventure.

Abuela Rosa

The wise matriarch who keeps the whole family together with her warmth.

Hector

The proud grandfather who runs the family mercado with passion.

Lalo

The lovable family dog who's always getting into mischief.
